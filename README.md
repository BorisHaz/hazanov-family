# Hazanov Family Memorial

A multilingual memorial website for the Hazanov family, victims of the Holocaust in Mariupol, 1941.

## Languages
English | Русский | עברית

## Pages
- **Home** — Landing page with navigation to memorial and children's book
- **Memorial** — Full adult memorial with the complete family story, organized by chapters
- **Children's Book** — Illustrated, page-by-page story for young readers

## Enabling GitHub Pages

1. Go to **Settings → Pages**
2. Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**
3. Choose **main** branch and **/ (root)** folder
4. Click **Save**

The site will be live at: `https://borishaz.github.io/hazanov-family/`

## About

This site commemorates the Hazanov family of Mariupol. On October 17–19, 1941, Nazi forces massacred the Jewish community of Mariupol at a site near Agrobaza. Of eight family members, only three survived.

Every year, on the second-to-last Sunday of October, people gather at the memorial site to remember.
